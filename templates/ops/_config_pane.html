Agree Threshold: max fractional difference for LLM/regex auto-accept (default 0.02 = 2%). Outlier Threshold: max deviation from trailing average before routing to review. Valid Range: values outside min/max are discarded before confidence scoring. Defaults are safe — only adjust after observing real extraction results.
